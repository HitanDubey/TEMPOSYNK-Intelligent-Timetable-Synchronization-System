ABOUT TEMPOSYNK

Redefining Academic Scheduling

We're on a mission to transform how educational institutions manage timetables through intelligent automation and algorithmic precision.

Our Mission

To eliminate scheduling conflicts and optimize resource utilization in educational institutions through intelligent, algorithm-driven timetable synchronization.

Our Vision

To become the global standard for academic scheduling, empowering institutions to focus on education while we handle the complexity of timetable management.

Our Story

Born from a passion for solving real-world scheduling challenges

TEMPOSYNK was founded with a simple yet powerful idea: scheduling shouldn't be a headache. What started as a university project at Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University has evolved into a comprehensive timetable synchronization system trusted by educational institutions.

Our journey began when we witnessed the countless hours administrators spent manually creating timetables, dealing with conflicts, and managing last-minute changes. We knew there had to be a better way.

Today, TEMPOSYNK combines algorithmic precision with user-friendly design to deliver optimal schedules in seconds. We're proud to serve institutions that value efficiency and innovation.
