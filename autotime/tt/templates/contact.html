Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about TEMPOSYNK

How does TEMPOSYNK generate timetables?

TEMPOSYNK uses advanced Python algorithms that analyze faculty availability, room capacity, course frequency, and scheduling constraints to generate optimal, conflict-free timetables automatically.

Is TEMPOSYNK free to use?

TEMPOSYNK offers flexible pricing plans. Contact us for detailed information about our subscription options tailored to your institution's needs.

Can I export timetables to different formats?

Yes! TEMPOSYNK supports export to PDF, Excel, Word, and CSV formats, making it easy to share and integrate with existing systems.

How do I handle faculty replacements?

TEMPOSYNK includes a professor replacement feature. Simply enter the absent professor's ID and replacement details, and the system automatically updates the timetable.

What support do you offer?

We provide email support, documentation, and technical assistance. Contact us at info@temposynk.io for any questions or issues.
