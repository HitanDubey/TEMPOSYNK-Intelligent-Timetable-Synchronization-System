GETTING STARTED

Your Journey to Smart Scheduling

Follow these simple steps to create your first timetable with TEMPOSYNK

1

Add Program

Navigate to "Add Program" and enter your academic programs with semester details. This defines the structure of your institution.

2

Add Faculty

Register all professors with their details - ID, name, course assignments, phone number, and email for contact.

3

Add Courses

Enter course codes, names, frequency per week, and associate them with specific programs and semesters.

4

Add Lecture Rooms

Register all lecture halls with their unique IDs and seating capacities for proper resource allocation.

5

Add Lab Rooms

Enter laboratory details including lab IDs and capacities for practical session scheduling.

6

Generate Timetable

Select program, semester, batches, and click generate. Watch our algorithm create optimal schedules!
